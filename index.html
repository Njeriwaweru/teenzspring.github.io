About Us

Teenzspring is an organization aimed to empower teenagers to become
wholesome adults who would be confident in who they are and consequently
inspire them to positively impact their environment.

Our Vision

To create a culture of transformed
teenagers.

Our Mission

We seek to bring the awareness of
identity and hope in teenagers
stirring up growth and change.

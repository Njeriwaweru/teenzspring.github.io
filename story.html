Our Story

Who we are

Teenzspring is an organization aimed to empower teenagers to become wholesome adults who would be confident in who they are and consequently inspire them to positively impact their environment.
According to Federation of Kenya Employees, Although the overall unemployment in Kenya is at 12.7 percent, Youth (15 â€“ 34 year olds), who form 35 percent of the Kenyan population, have the highest unemployment rate of 67 percent. Over one million young people enter into the labor market annually without any skills, some having either dropped out of school or completed school and not enrolled in any college.
This has warranted teenagers/youths to be in despair and lose faith in cultivating their skills because their future is not as certain as they expected. Despite the economic situation, Teenzspring is confident that our youth have the opportunity to become entrepreneurs and create numerous job opportunities. In light of this, we are geared towards teaching teenagers skills on how they can harness their creativity potential. We believe that youths who will work in developing a creative mindset will be entrepreneurs of all rounded sustainable enterprises.
A partnership with teenzspring produces all rounded and good performing students. We provide a platform whereby teenagers get to learn basic skills that will help them in navigating limitations imposed by their environment/background. We offer a program that will benefit students greatly. We acknowledge that it is important for every student to receive a wholesome buffeted education rich in growing them in their IQ and emotional intelligence.Our services are centered towards building emotional intelligence. This is a skill greatly ignored but that is pertinent in a society that seeks to see teenagers thrive.
